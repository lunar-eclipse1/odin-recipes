Mama's Perfect Lasagna

This recipe calls for the most delicious and homemade lasagna that is better than your average homecook meal, and it will make sure to call your mama up to reminise the old times. This recipe uses ingrediants already found in ypur fridge and pantry.

How to Make Lasagna

Making lasagna takes time and prep, but the results are well worth the wait. You will find deatiled ingrediants list and step-by-step instriction in the recipe below.

Ingredients

  • Meat: Lean ground beef and sweet italian sausage.
  • Onion and Garlic.
  • Tomato products: This recipe calls for a can of sundried tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Spices and seasonings: I like to use fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Sugar: the secrete ingrediant
  • Lasagna noodles: You can use store-bought or homemade.
  • Cheese: Parm, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese
  • Egg: Helps bind the ricotta

Lasagna Making Step-by-step

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
